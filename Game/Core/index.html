Pew Pew

A GAME FOR APCSP

by ben paulsen

Move with all four arrow keys.

Your lives are shown in the bottom left corner.
You start with only three -- protect them.

The circles are your enemies! Shoot them by holding down [SPACEBAR].
Some enemies shoot back. Dodge their bullets to avoid losing lives!
Colliding with enemies is also dangerous.
Most importantly -- destroy enemies before they get to the bottom!









Your score is shown in the top left, with a personal best displayed in red underneath.
You earn points when you destroy an enemy circle -- try to beat your highscore!

Occasionally, blinking green and yellow boxes will slowly drop; these are powerups.
They're helpful in a pinch -- collide your ship with them to attain a temporary buff.

The rest, you'll figure out yourself. Good luck!
